About Me

Native Clevelander. Typical Gryffindor. Loves being outdoors, jiu jitsu, cooking, reading, traveling, and problem-solving. Listens to a pretty eclectic assortment of music. Dances (badly). Addicted to black coffee. Delighted by the wild world of coding.
