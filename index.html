About Me

Native Clevelander. Typical Gryffindor. Loves being outdoors, jiu jitsu, cooking, reading, traveling, and problem-solving. Listens to a pretty eclectic assortment of music. Dances (badly). Addicted to black coffee. Delighted by this wild world of coding.

More seriously, I'm a full-stack programmer and process designer with four years of experience in business process analysis, ERP implementation, production planning, quality control, and technical sales. Following three years working in Japan, I completed my MBA at Case Western Reserve with an operations concentration. I am currently seeking opportunities to help design and create robust, sustainable solutions that fully satisfy requirements and that end users will enjoy working with.
